Son Goku

Anime: Dragon Ball Z / Dragon Ball Super

Role: Protagonist | Power Type: Ki, Saiyan Transformations

A cheerful Saiyan warrior who loves training and fighting strong opponents, constantly pushing his limits to protect Earth and his friends.

Baki Hanma

Anime: Baki the Grappler

Role: Protagonist | Power Type: MMA Fighter

Baki Hanma, son of Yujiro Hanma, is a master of many deadly martial arts and fights to surpass his father.

Naruto Uzumaki

Anime: Naruto / Naruto Shippuden

Role: Protagonist | Power Type: Ninjutsu, Chakra

Naruto is a determined ninja who dreams of becoming Hokage and earning the respect of his village.

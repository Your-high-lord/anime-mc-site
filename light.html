Light Yagami

A top‑tier student who discovers the Death Note and decides to become a god of a new world.

Allies and Rivals

  • L – the world‑famous detective determined to expose Kira.
  • Misa Amane – devoted supporter who also holds a Death Note.
  • Ryuk – the bored Shinigami who drops the notebook into the human world.

Key Themes

  • Justice twisted by absolute power.
  • Cat‑and‑mouse mind games and psychological warfare.
  • The cost of playing god over life and death.

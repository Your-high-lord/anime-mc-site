Alucard

The ultimate vampire weapon of the Hellsing Organization, reveling in battle against monsters and humans alike.

Allies and Enemies

  • Integra Hellsing – the master Alucard loyally serves.
  • Seras Victoria – a police officer turned vampire under Alucard.
  • Millennium and other supernatural threats that challenge Hellsing.

Power and Style

  • Regeneration, shapeshifting, and overwhelming vampiric strength.
  • Iconic red coat, hat, and dual guns.
  • A sadistic yet charismatic presence on the battlefield.

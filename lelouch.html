Lelouch vi Britannia

An exiled prince who gains the power of Geass and leads a masked rebellion as Zero.

Allies and Opponents

  • CC – the mysterious girl who grants Lelouch his Geass.
  • Suzaku Kururugi – childhood friend turned rival on the battlefield.
  • Black Knights – the resistance force led by Zero.

Strategy and Themes

  • High‑level tactics, gambits, and mecha warfare.
  • Moral ambiguity of revolution and sacrifice.
  • Balancing personal revenge with the hope for a better world.
